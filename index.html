A Safer Way to Travel

All aircrafts and trains are thoroughly cleaned and regularly disinfected to ensure the highest standards of cleanliness, with special emphasis on high-frequency touch points like door handles, tray tables and seats. The crew members are also well-trained in hygiene etiquettes and safe practices so that you can travel without worry.


Time-Saving and Convenient

you don’t even have to reach at the airport hours in advance. Just arrive 35-40 minutes before departure—depending on the airport and your flight brief— and head straight to your private aircraft. This is about as smooth as it gets!


Swiftness of Booking

For urgent office meetings or any medical emergencies, the swiftness with which you can be airborne is of prime importance. Charter flights can be booked at a very short notice with minimal formalities and paperwork. For frequent corporate fliers, membership packages are often also available for multiple group bookings during a year.


Customised Itineraries and Services

Not only do you get to select the perfect aircraft for your needs from a wide range of options, you have full control over your flight timings and itinerary. Fly on your own terms, at your preferred time, without any inconvenient layovers and stopovers. Chartered flights allow you to customize your journey down to the last detail.


Absolute Privacy

Whether you want to keep your travel discreet or conduct important business meetings while on the go, a charter flight offers you utmost peace and privacy. Besides, while flying alone or in small groups, you don't have to come in contact with strangers, flying as your co-passengers. It is an extravagance way beyond flying first class.
